About us

We are an international leader in human capital, bringing the best talent to your company according to your profile and needs, serving you for three decades.

Our services

Acording your needs, we can process visas / permits to workers that you have hired or we can recruit and select qualified workers. We understand the need to hire trained and willing workers. LaborMexy can coordinate interviews of prequalified workers by videoconferencing so your staff does not have to travel far. We regularly carry out guidance and training services for US companies, Canada and other countries, we have technology to ensure that your human resources offices can monitor recruitment to distance.

What can we do for you?

Recruitment of suitable personnel

Process work visas for the group you want to take you

Represent you before the consulate and the different agencies involved in the legal process of work visas

Selection of personnel who meet the qualifications you are looking for

Conduct interviews on your behalf, quizzes, tests and exams

Prequalify the selected personnel to see if they meet the conditions of the visa they are processing

Staff accommodation to the job site

Review of documents required for the processing and assembly of the file

Prepare staff for their consular interview

Processing of a labor petition before the corresponding instances / agencies by the employer or contractor

Analysis of the employer or labor study on requirements, costs and information of the process

Advice to the employer on the most convenient type of visa according to their needs

Rules, regulations and terms that employers submit to when taking hired people
